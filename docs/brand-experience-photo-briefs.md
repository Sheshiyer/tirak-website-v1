# Brand Experience Photo Briefs

> Atmospheric, scene-setting cinematic shots for each Tirak category. These are NOT persona portraits (see `vendor-persona-photo-briefs.md` for those). These are environmental, mood-setting images for OG cards, hero stills, experience card backgrounds, and marketing materials.

## Guidelines

- **No identifiable faces** in any hero/establishing shots
- **Aspect ratios:** 16:9 (hero backgrounds) + 3:4 (card backgrounds)
- **Min resolution:** 1920x1080 for hero usage
- **Style:** Cinematic photography — Apple TV+ travel documentary quality
- **Color grading:** Aligned with each category's HSL theme from CategoriesGrid
- **Format:** Each category has 3 prompt types: Hero/Establishing, Texture/Detail, Wide/Aerial

---

## 1. Culture

**Category Color:** `hsl(270, 65%, 55%)` — Deep Violet / Amber Gold

### 1.1 Hero / Establishing Shot

- **Subject:** The golden spires of Wat Phra Kaew at dawn, silhouetted against a deep violet-amber sky
- **Composition:** Wide 16:9 frame, temple complex fills lower two-thirds, sky dominates upper third with graduated color
- **Location:** Grand Palace complex, Bangkok
- **Time of Day:** Golden hour, first light — 6:15 AM
- **Mood:** Reverent, ancient, awe-inspiring, spiritual
- **Color Palette:** Deep amber gold, temple gold leaf reflections, violet-purple dawn sky, warm stone tones
- **Technical Notes:** Low angle to emphasize spire height. Lens flare from rising sun catching gold leaf. Shoot at f/8 for sharpness across depth. No people in foreground.

### 1.2 Texture / Detail Shot

- **Subject:** Close-up of intricate mosaic tilework on a temple wall — tiny glass and ceramic fragments forming a mythological figure
- **Composition:** 3:4 portrait frame, shallow depth of field (f/2.8), sharp center with dreamy bokeh edges
- **Location:** Wat Arun ceramic detail or Wat Pho mosaic wall
- **Time of Day:** Overcast midday for even, shadowless light on texture
- **Mood:** Meticulous, ancient craftsmanship, devotional detail
- **Color Palette:** Gold, deep blue, emerald green, ruby red mosaic fragments against warm sandstone
- **Technical Notes:** Macro lens or 85mm at minimum focus. Capture individual tile edges catching light. Avoid reflections from protective glass.

### 1.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Drone perspective over a sea of golden temple roofs in Chiang Mai's old city, morning mist threading between spires
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, elevated 45-degree angle, temple roofs cascading toward misty mountains in background
- **Location:** Chiang Mai Old City, aerial view toward Doi Suthep
- **Time of Day:** Early morning — 6:30 AM, mist still present
- **Mood:** Mystical, timeless, vast cultural landscape
- **Color Palette:** Gold temple roofs, green tree canopy, white morning mist, violet mountain silhouettes
- **Technical Notes:** Drone at 100m altitude. ND filter for slow shutter to blur mist movement. GPS coordinates for Chiang Mai Old City center.

## 2. Adventure

**Category Color:** `hsl(210, 70%, 50%)` — Ocean Blue / Vivid Teal

### 2.1 Hero / Establishing Shot

- **Subject:** Limestone karsts rising from turquoise Andaman Sea water, a longtail boat's wake cutting through the frame
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide cinematic, karsts in middle distance, water filling lower half, dramatic cloud formation above
- **Location:** Phang Nga Bay or Krabi coastline
- **Time of Day:** Late afternoon — 4 PM, sun backlighting karsts
- **Mood:** Vast, thrilling, pristine wilderness, invitation to explore
- **Color Palette:** Deep turquoise water, grey-green limestone, white boat wake, cobalt sky with cumulus clouds
- **Technical Notes:** Polarizing filter to cut water reflections and saturate blue. Longtail boat positioned at rule-of-thirds intersection. No people visible on boat (shoot from distance).

### 2.2 Texture / Detail Shot

- **Subject:** Underwater close-up of a coral reef wall — brain coral, sea fans, and a school of neon-blue damselfish
- **Composition:** 3:4 portrait, shallow DOF on coral with fish school in soft focus background, sunlight rays from surface above
- **Location:** Similan Islands or Koh Lipe reef
- **Time of Day:** 10 AM — sun high enough for light penetration
- **Mood:** Alive, vibrant, hidden underwater world, sensory immersion
- **Color Palette:** Electric blue fish, coral pink and orange, green sea fans, golden sunbeam rays through turquoise water
- **Technical Notes:** Underwater housing, wide-angle lens (10-17mm), strobe for color restoration at depth. Shoot upward to catch sun rays.

### 2.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Drone shot of a pristine crescent beach with jungle-covered headlands on each side, crystal water revealing reef patterns beneath
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, directly overhead or high-angle, jungle green frames turquoise water and white sand arc
- **Location:** Maya Bay (Koh Phi Phi Leh) or Railay Beach
- **Time of Day:** Midday for maximum water color saturation
- **Mood:** Paradise found, adventure awaits, pristine wilderness
- **Color Palette:** Emerald jungle canopy, white sand crescent, graduated turquoise-to-deep-blue water, reef shadows
- **Technical Notes:** Drone at 150m for maximum bay coverage. Shoot when no boats present. HDR for dynamic range between dark jungle and bright sand.

## 3. Wellness

**Category Color:** `hsl(155, 65%, 45%)` — Serene Green / Soft Jade

### 3.1 Hero / Establishing Shot

- **Subject:** A lotus pond at a tropical spa, steam rising from the water surface, frangipani petals floating, bamboo screen in background
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, low angle from water surface level, lotus blooms sharp in foreground, spa pavilion softly blurred behind
- **Location:** Chiva-Som-style tropical spa garden, Hua Hin or Chiang Mai
- **Time of Day:** Dawn — 6 AM, soft diffused light, steam visible
- **Mood:** Deep calm, restoration, sanctuary, nature healing
- **Color Palette:** Jade green water, soft pink lotus, white frangipani, warm bamboo tones, misty white steam
- **Technical Notes:** Low tripod at water level. Long exposure (1/4s) for silky steam. 85mm for compression of background elements.

### 3.2 Texture / Detail Shot

- **Subject:** Hands placing a hot herbal compress (luk pra kob) on folded white linen towels beside a stone mortar with fresh lemongrass and turmeric
- **Composition:** 3:4 portrait, overhead flat-lay angle, shallow DOF on compress with herbs blurred at edges
- **Location:** Thai spa treatment room
- **Time of Day:** Soft indoor lighting, warm tone
- **Mood:** Sensory, therapeutic, handcrafted, aromatic
- **Color Palette:** White linen, turmeric gold, green lemongrass, warm wood tones, cloth-wrapped herbal ball
- **Technical Notes:** 50mm macro, f/4 for selective focus on compress texture. Natural window light from one side. Hands cropped to show only wrists — no face.

### 3.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Elevated view of a yoga deck cantilevered over a jungle valley, two yoga mats laid out (unoccupied), morning mist in valley below
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, drone at 30m altitude looking down at platform edge, jungle canopy stretching to misty horizon
- **Location:** Northern Thailand jungle retreat, Chiang Dao or Pai
- **Time of Day:** 6:30 AM, mist in valley, golden first light
- **Mood:** Elevation, peace, communion with nature, spaciousness
- **Color Palette:** Teak wood deck, jade-green canopy, white mist, warm golden light on deck surface
- **Technical Notes:** Drone low enough to see deck texture detail. Two mats suggest human presence without showing people. Morning mist mandatory for mood.

## 4. Nightlife

**Category Color:** `hsl(340, 70%, 55%)` — Electric Pink / Neon Teal

### 4.1 Hero / Establishing Shot

- **Subject:** Bangkok rooftop bar at twilight — the city skyline glittering behind an infinity pool edge, neon signage reflected in water
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, low angle from pool edge, skyline fills upper two-thirds, water reflections in lower third
- **Location:** Lebua or similar Silom/Sathorn rooftop, Bangkok
- **Time of Day:** Blue hour — 7 PM, just after sunset, city lights on but sky still has color
- **Mood:** Electric anticipation, glamorous, elevated, urban energy
- **Color Palette:** Deep teal sky, neon pink and amber reflections, golden city lights, dark water with color streaks
- **Technical Notes:** Wide angle (16-35mm), long exposure (2-4s) for light trails and smooth water. Tripod essential. No people in frame.

### 4.2 Texture / Detail Shot

- **Subject:** Close-up of a craft cocktail on a dark bar counter — smoke wafting from a glass, ice refracting colored light, condensation drops on glass surface
- **Composition:** 3:4 portrait, eye-level with drink, shallow DOF (f/1.8), bokeh of bar lights behind
- **Location:** Speakeasy bar in Thonglor or Chinatown
- **Time of Day:** Evening — ambient bar lighting only
- **Mood:** Intimate, mysterious, sensory, craft excellence
- **Color Palette:** Amber liquid, neon pink light on glass surface, teal bar lighting, dark moody shadows, golden highlights
- **Technical Notes:** 85mm f/1.4, natural bar lighting only (no flash). Capture smoke trail mid-movement. Focus on condensation drops.

### 4.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Drone view of Sukhumvit road at night — a river of red and white car lights, rooftop bars glowing on adjacent buildings, Bangkok skyline stretching to horizon
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, drone at 200m looking down the road axis, light trails forming lines that converge at vanishing point
- **Location:** Sukhumvit Road corridor, Bangkok
- **Time of Day:** 9 PM — full darkness, maximum neon and traffic
- **Mood:** Urban pulse, electric energy, city that never sleeps
- **Color Palette:** Red/white traffic streams, neon signs (pink, teal, amber), dark building silhouettes, orange sodium streetlights
- **Technical Notes:** Long exposure (8-15s) from drone with stabilization for light trail effect. Shoot on weekend night for maximum traffic volume.

- **Mood:** Curated nostalgia, editorial cool, Bangkok character, Instagram-ready
- **Color Palette:** Pastel pink, mint green, faded yellow facades, warm amber light, aged wood and iron details

### 5.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Bird's eye view of a Bangkok concept mall courtyard — geometric landscaping, a reflecting pool, scattered umbrellas over cafe tables
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, directly overhead drone shot, geometric patterns formed by architecture, landscaping, and furniture

- **Mood:** Designed living, urban oasis, contemporary Thai style

## 6. Cinema

**Category Color:** `hsl(250, 65%, 55%)` — Deep Indigo / Tungsten Warm

### 6.1 Hero / Establishing Shot

- **Subject:** Interior of a vintage Thai cinema — rows of red velvet seats leading to a glowing screen, projector beam visible in dusty air
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, centered perspective down the aisle, vanishing point at the screen, projector beam diagonal through frame
- **Location:** Scala cinema (historic) or similar vintage theater
- **Time of Day:** Dark interior — screen provides primary light source
- **Mood:** Nostalgia, cinematic magic, cathedral of stories, film noir warmth
- **Color Palette:** Deep red velvet, tungsten gold projector beam, blue-white screen glow, dark indigo shadows
- **Technical Notes:** Wide angle (16mm), long exposure for projector beam visibility. No audience in seats. Screen can show solid color or static.

### 6.2 Texture / Detail Shot

- **Subject:** Close-up of 35mm film reels and canisters stacked on a wooden shelf, warm sidelight catching metallic surfaces and film sprocket edges
- **Composition:** 3:4 portrait, side angle with dramatic sidelight, shallow DOF (f/2.0) on front reel with others falling to bokeh
- **Location:** Film archive or vintage cinema projection room
- **Time of Day:** Indoor — single tungsten light source from side
- **Mood:** Tactile nostalgia, analog warmth, craft of filmmaking, preserved memory
- **Color Palette:** Silver metallic reels, warm tungsten highlights, dark wood shelf, amber film canister labels
- **Technical Notes:** 85mm at f/2. Single practical light source (tungsten bulb) for dramatic shadow. Capture dust particles in light beam.

### 6.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Rooftop outdoor cinema setup — a large inflatable screen, string lights, cushioned seating area, Bangkok skyline twinkling behind
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, elevated angle from adjacent building or drone, screen and seating area as a glowing island against dark cityscape
- **Location:** Rooftop venue in Bangkok's creative district
- **Time of Day:** Night — 8:30 PM, screen lit, string lights glowing
- **Mood:** Magical, communal, romantic, open-air enchantment
- **Color Palette:** Warm string light glow, cool screen light, city amber backdrop, dark blue night sky
- **Technical Notes:** High ISO (3200+), wide aperture for available light. Long exposure for smooth string light glow. Empty seats to suggest anticipation.

## 7. Events

### 7.1 Hero / Establishing Shot

- **Subject:** Hundreds of sky lanterns rising over a river at Yi Peng festival, their warm glow reflected in the water surface below
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, lanterns filling the sky from bottom-right to upper-left in a rising stream, river reflections below

- **Mood:** Transcendent, collective wish-making, spiritual spectacle, awe
- **Color Palette:** Warm orange lantern glow, dark night sky, amber water reflections, occasional red and gold lanterns
- **Technical Notes:** Wide angle (24mm), f/4, ISO 1600, 1/60s. Capture at peak release moment. Focus on lanterns mid-air. No faces identifiable at this scale.

- **Subject:** Close-up of a Muay Thai fighter's wrapped hands resting on the ropes of a ring, gloves hanging beside them, stadium lights flaring behind

- **Mood:** Raw power, tradition, pre-fight tension, athletic ritual
- **Color Palette:** White hand wraps, red ring ropes, warm skin tones, golden stadium light bokeh, dark background

### 7.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Subject:** Drone view of Songkran water festival chaos on Silom Road — the street transformed into a river of celebration, water spray catching sunlight in rainbow arcs
- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, overhead drone looking straight down, the road as a packed river of color and water spray

- **Mood:** Explosive joy, collective celebration, controlled chaos, pure fun
- **Color Palette:** Bright rainbow spray, colorful clothing crowds, yellow road markings, green tree canopy edges, white water spray

### 8.3 Wide / Aerial Shot

- **Composition:** 16:9 wide, directly overhead, boats arranged organically in canal, colors creating a natural still-life pattern

- **Mood:** Abundance, color explosion, living tradition, Thai way of life

- **Technical Notes:** Drone at 30m for boat detail. Shoot when boats are clustered for maximum color density. No individual vendors identifiable at this altitude.

- **Midday shots:** Adventure 2.2, Adventure 2.3, Lifestyle 5.2, Lifestyle 5.3 (10 AM-2 PM)

- **Anytime (controlled):** Culture 1.2, Wellness 3.2, Cinema 6.2, Food 8.2

### Equipment
- Camera: Full-frame mirrorless (Sony A7IV or equivalent)
- Lenses: 16-35mm f/2.8, 50mm f/1.4, 85mm f/1.4, 100mm macro
- Drone: DJI Mavic 3 Pro (Hasselblad camera)
- Filters: Circular polarizer, ND64, ND1000
- Lighting: Portable LED panel, reflector (texture shots only)
- Underwater: Housing rated to 40m, wide-angle dome port, dual strobes

### Post-Processing
- Color grade each set to match the category's HSL color theme
- Culture: warm amber lift, violet shadow toning
- Adventure: teal-blue push, saturated greens
- Wellness: desaturated greens, soft highlights, lifted blacks
- Nightlife: neon color push, crushed blacks, teal-orange split tone
- Lifestyle: warm editorial tone, slightly lifted blacks, pastel enhancement
- Cinema: tungsten warmth, deep indigo shadows, film grain overlay
- Events: warm orange push, high saturation, slight vignette
- Food & Drink: warm golden highlights, rich shadows, high clarity on food texture
