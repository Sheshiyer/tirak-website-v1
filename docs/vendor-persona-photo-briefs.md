## General Photo Guidelines

- **Ethnicity:** All personas are Thai. Authentic Southeast Asian features.
- **Age:** Mid-20s to mid-30s range — young professional energy.
- **Expression:** Natural, genuine smiles or candid mid-action. No stiff posed shots.
- **Lighting:** Category-appropriate (golden hour for temples, neon for nightlife, soft diffused for wellness, bright tropical for adventure).
- **Aspect ratio:** 3:4 portrait orientation (matches the card component `aspect-[3/4]`).
- **Resolution:** High-res, at least 600x800px for web display.
- **Style:** Realistic photography, not illustration. Think editorial travel magazine quality — National Geographic meets Monocle.
- **Avoid:** Stock photo feel, overly filtered/saturated colors, Western-looking models, generic backgrounds, watermarks.
- **Color grading:** Warm and natural for most categories. Teal/amber for nightlife. Bright and vivid for adventure. Muted earth tones for wellness.
